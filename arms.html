ARMS


Arms. The muscle group that seems to be the most overtrained. "Hey you want to hit legs today?" "Nah bro
let's hit arms." While training arms are very nice, just know that it's not everything. There is more to working
out then solely hitting biceps and triceps. And for the girls, don't be scared to work out your arms because
you think it's gonna make you too big. I promise you it won't. Also, although these aren't necessarily connected
with your arms, I will include some trap exercises as well, as that is a muscle group that you shouldn't neglect.
