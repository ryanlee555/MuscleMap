ABOUT


Hi! My name is Ryan, and I have been actively going to the gym since 2020. I made this website because
going to the gym for the first time can be scary. I remember my first time entering the gym, nervous and scared that
others were judging me. I didn't have a structured workout routine, and for the first week, I didn't know what to do.

However, as time went on, I began to find a groove, and figured out which exercises best suited me. Even to this day, I
make changes in my workouts, and that's totally okay! The most important part of going to the gym is starting. This
website lists many of my personal favorite exercises that I do, all categorized into its own muscle groups. Note that
the workouts listed aren't the only ones you can do; like I said, these are just some of my favorite ones. Hopefully,
this website can get you started on your gym journey. Best of luck!
