CHEST


The chest can be separated into three groups: upper, lower, and middle. The scientific terms for these respective groups
are the: clavicular head, abdominal head, and sternal head. It is crucial to hit all three parts of the chest in order to achieve
a symmetrical and well-developed chest. You don't want a crazy upper chest with no lower chest to show, right? It is most
commonly advised to have at least three chest exercises for a workout, one for each part. Below are some exercises you
can choose to hit all three parts of the chest.




UPPER CHEST



Incline Dumbbell Press

Incline Barbell Press

Low-to-High Cable Fly



Incline Dumbbell Fly

Incline Pushup



LOWER CHEST







Dips

High-to-Low Cable Fly

Seated Dips




MIDDLE CHEST






Dumbbell Press

Barbell Bench Press

Machine Flies




Middle Cable Flies

Diamond Push Ups
