QUADS/ HAMSTRINGS


The quads and hamstrings are the two main components that make up the leg. In other words, they're
very important to work out and build if you don't want to look like you have bones as legs. The quads
are the front of your thigh, while the hamstrings are in the back. Leg muscles are the root of explosiveness,
athleticism, and power. Let me show you some great exercises you can do to work these out effectively.
