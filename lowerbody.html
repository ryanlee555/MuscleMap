LOWER BODY


The lower body is composed of four primary muscle groups: quads, hamstrings, glutes/ abductors, and calves.
Even though everyone hates it, hitting legs is crucial, as it builds and develops foundational strength and supports overall
body stability. No one wants to have a huge upper body and no leg muscle, just look at Larry the Lobster. The dropdown
menu shows a variety of exercises you can do to hit each part of your legs effectively. Don't skip leg day.
