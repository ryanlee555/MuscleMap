BACK


The back, similar to the chest, is one of the largest muscle groups that has three main regions:
upper, middle, and lats. Hitting each region consistently is very important as it develops your
back evenly. Working out the middle and upper back will give you a strong, wide base on the top region of
your back, while hitting your lats will give you that dorito shape. Below are some of the workouts you can do to hit
each part of your back.




UPPER BACK



Chin Up (Close grip)


Face Pulls


Bent Over Rows




Chest Supported Rows


Reverse Pec Deck Flies




MIDDLE BACK



Seated Cable Rows


T-bar Rows


Deadlift




LATS



Wide Grip Pull Ups

Lat Pulldowns




Single Arm Pulldowns
