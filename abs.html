ABS


"Abs are made in the kitchen, not the gym". While this saying definitely has some truth to it, working out your
abs are still important if you want that defined looking six-pack. Not only that, but ab workouts strengthen your
core, building a more stable and balanced body. There are a plethora of different ab exercises you can do if you look
it up, but the most important two are any crunch and raise movement. Based off my experience, any other is an excess of
volume, and less is more for abs. Nevertheless, I'll show you my personal favorite ab workouts you can do to not only strengthen
your core, but show off that six pack as well.
