UPPER BODY


The upper body is composed of four primary muscle groups: chest, back, shoulders/ delts, and arms (biceps and triceps).
The chest, shoulder, and tricep muscles are integral to pushing movements, while the back and bicep muscles are essential for
pulling actions. Everyone wants a big, jacked upper body, and for the girlies who are scared to hit it because you think
it will make you look too bulky, it won't. Don't worry. Don't just work out your legs girls. The dropdown menu offers a detailed
overview of each muscle group, along with a variety of exercises designed to effectively target and strengthen those muscles.
